This creates a massive market failure. The $16 trillion AI economy - agents managing workflows, making decisions, coordinating tasks - is completely locked out of the $5.6 trillion e-commerce market. Agents can't buy data, can't access paid APIs, can't procure tools they need.

We built the solution: x402 Everything - complete payment infrastructure for autonomous agents.

Here's how it works: First, payment discovery using the HTTP 402 protocol. Agents can read machine-formatted payment requirements. No human-readable websites needed.
